Notes:
- Teensy 4.0 uses MIMXRT1062DVJ6A, Teensy 4.1 uses MIMXRT1062DVJ6B. Both are the industrial/automotive-grade i.MX RT1062 crossover processor (600 MHz Cortex-M7).
- Two datasheet variants are kept: consumer temperature grade (`IMXRT1060CEC`) and industrial temperature grade (`IMXRT1060IEC`). Teensy uses the industrial-grade part.
- NXP has since published RM Rev. 3 (covering i.MX RT1064 too, ~30 MB via Farnell mirror). PJRC's Rev. 2 is the widely-cited copy in the Teensy community and matches the silicon on all shipping Teensy 4.x boards.

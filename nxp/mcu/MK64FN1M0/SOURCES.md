Notes:
- Teensy 3.5 uses the MK64FX512VMD12 variant of this family (1 MB flash / 256 KB SRAM / 120 MHz Cortex-M4F, 144-LQFP). Datasheet + RM cover the full K64F sub-family.
- The 1N83J errata covers mask set 1N83J for K24/K63/K64 including MK64FN1M0VMD12/VLQ12/VDC12/VLL12/CAJ12 and MK64FX512VMD12/VLQ12/VDC12/VLL12.
- NXP direct URL `https://www.nxp.com/docs/en/reference-manual/K64P144M120SF5RM.pdf` returned a redirect stub (10 bytes) at fetch time. PJRC hosts a verbatim copy of the NXP-published RM (Rev. 3+, ~14 MB).

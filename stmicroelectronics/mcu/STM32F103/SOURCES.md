Notes:
- `datasheet.pdf` covers the STM32F103x8/xB medium-density devices (representative
  of the very common `STM32F103C8` "Blue Pill" MCU). Other density variants
  (low/high/XL) have separate datasheets on st.com.
- `reference-manual.pdf` is **RM0008**, which is the single reference manual
  covering the entire STM32F101/F102/F103/F105/F107 family.
- `errata.pdf` is **ES096**, covering STM32F101x8/B, STM32F102x8/B and
  STM32F103x8/B medium-density device limitations.

Notes:
- **STM32H747** is the dual-core (Cortex-M7 @ 480 MHz + Cortex-M4 @ 240 MHz)
  chip used on the Arduino GIGA R1 WiFi.
- `datasheet.pdf` is the STM32H747xI/G family datasheet.
- `reference-manual.pdf` is **RM0399**, covering STM32H745/755 and STM32H747/757.
- `errata.pdf` is **ES0445**, the shared errata sheet for the same four device
  families.

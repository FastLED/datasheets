- **Notes:** English-language datasheet mirrored by Adafruit. LPD8806 is a
  3-channel 7-bit constant-current LED PWM driver used in early SPI-clocked
  addressable LED strips. Vendor origin sometimes attributed to Chongqing
  Titan Micro / Shenzhen LPD — kept under the `titanmicro` slug here.
